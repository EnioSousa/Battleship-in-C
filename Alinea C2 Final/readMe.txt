How to compile:
$make clean
$make

How to run:

In one shell do
$./main 1 "map size"

In other shell do
$./main 2 "map size"

Or

In one shell do
$./main 2 "map size"

In other shell do
$./main 1 "map size"

Note: If you initiate player 1 first, then it shall be the first to play
otherwise player 2 will be the first
